About Edmonton Gallery Walk

The Edmonton Gallery Walk, continuing a tradition that began in 1981, promotes art and artists within our community, focusing especially on work by Canadian artists.

The Gallery Walk is located west of the downtown core in the 124 Street area, a vital business community with many quality restaurants, gift and clothing shops, bookstores, live theatre venues and specialty businesses.
