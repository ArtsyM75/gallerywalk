About Edmonton Gallery Walk

The Edmonton Gallery Walk, continuing a tradition that began in 1981, promotes art and artists within our community, focusing especially on work by Canadian artists.

The Gallery Walk is located west of the downtown core in the 124 Street area, a vital business community with many quality restaurants, gift and clothing shops, bookstores, live theatre venues and specialty businesses in its two block area.

Although formal unguided Gallery Walks are planned three times per year, we encourage group walks throughout the year, and will be happy to assist in the planning of any gallery walk for schools, conventions, or groups in general.

Please contact any of the member galleries if you are planning a visit to find out specific information about upcoming exhibitions.

Member Galleries

Our member galleries in the 124 Street area collaborate to present coordinated exhibitions and events.

For current gallery listings and exhibition information, please contact individual galleries directly or check back for updates to our website.

Gallery Walk Events

We organize three formal gallery walks per year, featuring special exhibitions coordinated among our member galleries.

Group walks are welcome throughout the year. Contact any member gallery to arrange visits for schools, conventions, or community groups.
