About Edmonton Gallery Walk

Since 1981, the Edmonton Gallery Walk has been a cornerstone of the city's vibrant arts community, promoting Canadian artists and fostering cultural enrichment.

Located in the 124 Street area, between 103 Avenue and 107 Avenue, our gallery walk event brings together art enthusiasts, collectors, and curious visitors in a dynamic neighborhood filled with restaurants and boutiques within a walkable distance.

Visit the member galleries outside of official Gallery Walk's for regular exhibitions, artist talks, and special events that celebrate the best of Canadian contemporary and traditional art.
