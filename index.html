About Edmonton Gallery Walk

The Edmonton Gallery Walk, continuing a tradition that began in 1981, promotes art and artists within our community, focusing especially on work by Canadian artists.

The Gallery Walk is located west of the downtown core in the 124 Street area, a vital business community with many quality restaurants, gift and clothing shops, bookstores, live theatre venues and specialty businesses in its two block area.

Although formal unguided Gallery Walks are planned twice per year, we encourage group walks throughout the year, and will be happy to assist in the planning of any gallery walk for schools, conventions, or groups in general.

Please contact any of the member galleries if you are planning a visit to find out specific information about upcoming exhibitions.
