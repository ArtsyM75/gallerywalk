About Edmonton Gallery Walk

Since 1981, the Edmonton Gallery Walk has been a cornerstone of the city's vibrant arts community, promoting Canadian artists and fostering cultural enrichment.

Located in the 124 Street district, our gallery walk brings together art enthusiasts, collectors, and curious visitors in a dynamic neighborhood filled with restaurants, boutiques, and creative spaces.

Join us for regular exhibitions, artist talks, and special events that celebrate the best of contemporary and traditional art.
